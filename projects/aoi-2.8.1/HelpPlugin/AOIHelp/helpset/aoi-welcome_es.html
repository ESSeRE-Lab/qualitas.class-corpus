Welcome to Art Of Illusion!


In this help, we’ll try to answer your questions, whether they are as simple as “where do I start”, or something much more involved such as “how do I create depth-of-field blur?”.


The standard help topics cover the following:


  • Overview
    A short summary of AOI (Art Of Illusion) and what it can do.

  • Getting Started
    A quick overview of the AOI basics.

  • How To
    Short explanation of how to do particular tasks.

  • Getting further help
    Links to further help information. These include downloadable resources as well as online resources. This section will be different between AOI installations, due to differences in what different users install.

  • Reference
    A reference guide for AOI commands.

Since AOI help is dynamic, there may be further topics in your help, depending on what resources you may have installed.
